Golden Retriever

 The Golden Retriever is a sturdy, muscular dog of medium size, famous for the dense, lustrous coat of gold that
gives the breed its name. The broad head, with its friendly and intelligent eyes, short ears, and straight muzzle,
is a breed hallmark.

German Shepherd

 Generally considered dogkind's finest all-purpose worker, the German Shepherd Dog is a large, agile, muscular
dog of noble character and high intelligence. Loyal, confident, courageous, and steady, the German Shepherd is
truly a dog lover's delight.

Chow Chow

 Chows are powerful, compactly built dogs standing as high as 20 inches at the shoulder. Their distinctive traits
include a lion's-mane ruff around the head and shoulders; a blue-black tongue; deep-set almond eyes that add to
a scowling, snobbish expression; and a stiff-legged gait.
